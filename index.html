Why Jaipur Needs RO Water Purifiers

Understanding the water quality challenges in the Pink City

High TDS Levels

Jaipur's groundwater has TDS levels ranging from 800-2000 ppm, far exceeding the safe limit of 500 ppm recommended by authorities.

Hard Water

Calcium and magnesium deposits make water hard, causing scale buildup in pipes and appliances, and affecting skin and hair health.

Contamination Risk

Old pipelines and sewage mixing pose risks of bacterial contamination, making purification essential for sage drinking water.

Seasonal Variations

Water quality fluctuates significantly during monsoon and summer, requiring consistent purification throughout the year.
